About Me

Hi! I'm a software engineer currently located in the San Francisco Bay Area. Prior to becoming a software engineer, I worked as an audio engineer and studio technician at NBC Sports. I attended University of California at Davis and have a degree in Managerial Economics.

My interest in technology and web/mobile applications stemmed from realizing its impact on sports fans while I worked at NBC Sports. Mobile and web apps have improved the fan experience by helping fans engage with each other and by helping professional athletes connect with fans. This realization inspired me to learn more about programming and to eventually become a software engineer. I enjoy the challenges of problem solving, I love camaraderie when collaborating with other engineers, and most of all I am passionate about building applications that can have a positive impact on users.

When I have free time I love to do high intensity interval training workouts, learn new sous vides receipes, and study the NBA salary cap.

Better Sports - Frontend Engineer

A eSports and fantasy sports iOS app


App Store download

Technologies: JavaScript, React Native, GraphQL

Key product features include: React Native Navigation for login/logout, scrolling vertically to select different game events, swiping between game props, submitting user selections to backend via GraphQL mutations
